# Insurons Consumer User Guide

## Welcome to Insurons

Insurons is a modern insurance marketplace that lets you compare quotes from 50+ carriers, connect with licensed agents, and manage your entire insurance portfolio — all in one place.

---

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit **insurons.com** and click **Get Started**
2. Enter your name, email, and create a password
3. Select your role: **Consumer / Policyholder**
4. Check your email for a verification link and click it
5. You're in!

**Referral bonus:** If someone shared a referral link with you (URL contains `?ref=CODE`), you'll automatically receive **$10 credit** on signup.

### Logging In

- Go to **insurons.com/login**
- Enter your email and password
- Forgot your password? Click "Forgot Password" to receive a reset email

## Getting Insurance Quotes

### The Quote Calculator

The quote calculator is the fastest way to compare insurance rates. No login required.

**Step 1 — Choose insurance type**
Select from 40+ insurance products across these categories:
- **Personal Auto:** Auto, motorcycle, boat, RV, classic car
- **Property:** Homeowners, renters, condo, flood, landlord, mobile home
- **Life:** Term, whole, universal, variable, final expense
- **Health:** Individual, family, short-term, dental, vision, Medicare
- **Disability:** Long-term, short-term, long-term care, critical illness
- **Commercial:** BOP, general liability, workers comp, professional liability, cyber, D&O

**Step 2 — Enter your ZIP code**
Type your ZIP code (auto-complete helps you find it). This determines available carriers and state-specific rates.

**Step 3 — Answer a few questions**
Based on your insurance type, you'll see relevant fields:
- *Auto:* Vehicle year, make, model
- *Homeowners:* Home value, year built, square footage
- *Life:* Date of birth, smoker status, coverage amount
- *Health:* Household size, health rating
- *Commercial:* Business type, annual revenue, employees, years in business

**Step 4 — Choose coverage level**
Pick from Basic, Standard (most popular), or Premium.

**Step 5 — View your quotes**
See side-by-side quotes from multiple carriers with:
- Monthly and annual premiums
- Premium breakdown (base rate, coverage factor, state factor, policy fee, discount)
- Carrier AM Best ratings
- Coverage level descriptions
- Savings comparison (cheapest vs. most expensive)

Sort results by: Best Match, Price (Low to High), Price (High to Low), Lowest Deductible, or Carrier Rating.

### Saving Your Quote

Enter your contact information (name, email, phone) to:
- Save quotes to your account
- Get matched with a licensed agent in your area
- Receive follow-up with personalized options

### Quote Drafts

If you're logged in, your progress is automatically saved. Come back anytime and pick up where you left off — even across devices.

## My Applications

Track your insurance applications through every stage:

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Draft** | Application started but not yet submitted |
| **Submitted** | Application sent to the carrier for review |
| **Underwriting** | Carrier is reviewing your application |
| **Approved** | Application approved — ready to bind |
| **Bound** | Policy is active — coverage has started |

Each application shows:
- Carrier name and product
- Premium amount
- Coverage details
- Application timeline and notes
- Documents (upload and download)

### E-Signature

When your agent sends an application for signing:
1. You'll receive an email with a signing link
2. Click the link (no login required)
3. Review the application details, coverages, and insured items
4. Type your name and draw your signature on the canvas
5. Submit — you'll see a confirmation with your reference number

Your agent is automatically notified when you sign.

## Claims

### Filing a Claim

1. Go to **Claims** in the sidebar
2. Click **File New Claim**
3. Select the policy you're filing against
4. Describe what happened and upload supporting documents (photos, police reports, etc.)
5. Submit

### Claim Status Tracking

| Status | What It Means |
|--------|---------------|
| **Reported** | Claim filed and received |
| **Under Review** | Agent/carrier reviewing your claim |
| **Investigating** | Detailed investigation in progress |
| **Approved** | Claim approved for payment |
| **Denied** | Claim denied (with explanation) |
| **Settled** | Payment issued |
| **Closed** | Claim fully resolved |

Each claim includes:
- Activity timeline (every status change, note, and document)
- Document uploads (add more evidence anytime)
- Notes from your agent

## Tips for Getting the Best Rates

1. **Compare multiple quotes** — Rates vary significantly between carriers
2. **Bundle policies** — Many carriers offer multi-policy discounts
3. **Choose the right coverage level** — Don't over-insure or under-insure
4. **Maintain good credit** — Credit score impacts insurance rates in most states
5. **Ask about discounts** — Your agent can find discounts you may qualify for
6. **Review annually** — Rates change; re-quote every year at renewal time
